## Job Interviews

### Technical

*   How does the Infrastructure / Archtiecture looks like?
*   If I could start today and immediately work on a specific topic, wht would produe the biggest benefits / fix the most issues?
*   If you were to hire me now, where would you best utilize my skills?
*   What do you think would be the biggest difficulty for me in this position?

### Work Life Balance

*   How does the on-call schedulke / staffing looks like?
*   How does the company deal with Remote Work or Flexible Working Hours? Related, are there cour hours when everyone has to be available?
*   Are there Quite Periods when work slows down or is the volume of work contantly high?

### Company

*   In the Team I will be partof, how long have the senior and junior members been there?
*   Does the Position offer opportunities for advancement, training or mentoring?
*   If you had a free pass to change one thing in the company, no questions asked, what would it be?
*   Let's say everything goes well and I get the job. What would be "success" for you in this position in 6 / 12 months and 3 years?
*   What do you like best about \[company\]? Related, what do you like the least?

### Career and Application

*   How do you think I could be a better candidate for this position?
*   Is there anything about my application that worries you?

## Be Ready to Answer

*   Why are you applying / why are you looking now?
*   What is / are your biggest weakness
*   Why do you want to work at \[company\]?
*   What's your expectations of salary / total compensation?
*   What does a typical day look like at your current job?
*   Do you work overtime?
*   Have you visited our website?
