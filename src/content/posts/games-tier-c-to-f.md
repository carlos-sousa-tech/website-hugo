## Cats Hidden around the World

**Type:**

> Cats, Hidden Object, Cozy

**Description:**

> Find 2000 cats hiding in 20 iconic locations such as Rio de Janeiro, Istanbul or Tokyo in this cute hand-drawn hidden object game by Travellin Cats - the original viral hidden cats game creator! Can you find them all?

**Quick Review:**


> I can give it a positive review if you enjoy hidden object games and cats.  
> That is it. The game is very short, even though there are 2000 cats to find.  
>   
> This is probably more fun for a small child / kid then for an adult.  
> Still, the game is "good" in the sense that it does what it wants to do, very nice artwork, good-ish sound design, price also fair for what it offers, and the same can be said about the other games on the same type from the same developer.  
>   
> Therefore, if you like to find cats in images, go for it, if you have a child, also easy, else, maybe skip this one.

## Serial Cleaner

**Type:**

> Stealth, Action, Indie

**Description:**

> Slip on your flares, grab your shades and hop in your station wagon, it's time to work… as a mob professional cleaner. A good cleaner never gets caught, so you have to make sure you get in, clean up and get out without leaving a trace of evidence in this stylish award winning 2D action-stealth game.


**Quick Review:**

> A very simple, stealth-light game.  
> The challenge isn't that high, but it also doesn't overstay it's welcome.  
>   
> The visual style and sound design are nice, but nothing that will blow your mind.  
>   
> As the game says itself, you cleaning up after murders - nice movie references are present, the story is very much skip-able, and getting 100% achievements isn't that hard, taking between 4 -8 hours, so use that for your money / fun ratio.  
>   
> All in all, I would still recommend the game mainly for any of those times where you just want a quick game instead of a time sink and don't want to stress out about min-maxing gameplay, but also don't want something that is too passive.  

**Last Played:**

> 05/2024
